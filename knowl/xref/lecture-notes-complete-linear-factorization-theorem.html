Theorem A.5.5.

The complete linear factorization of a degree \(n\) polynomial function \(p(x)\) with leading coefficient \(a_n\) is given by the formula
\begin{equation*} p(x) = a_n (x - c_1)(x - c_2)\cdots(x - c_{n-1})(x - c_n), \end{equation*}
where \(c_1\text{,}\) \(c_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(c_{n-1}\text{,}\) \(c_n\) are the \(n\) (not necessarily distinct) solutions to the equation \(p(x) = 0\text{.}\)
